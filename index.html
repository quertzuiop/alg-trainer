Getting started

Choose a set of algorithms you want to train. You can choose between OLL and PLL.

After you choose a set, you will be presented with a list of cases. You can then select the cases you want to train.

When you are ready, click the "Done" button. You will then be presented with a timer and a scramble. You can start the timer by pressing the spacebar.

After you finish a solve, you can see your times and the cases you have solved. You can also delete the last solve if you made a mistake.
